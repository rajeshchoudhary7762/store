How It Works

Create An Account

Don't miss out on the chance to obtain the items you've always wanted by signing up now! Our platform hosts three fresh auctions every day, featuring only the finest luxury brands with brand-new, certified items. What's more, each auction commences at a starting price of zero dollars, and there's no minimum reserve price. Bid with confidence as each auction runs for a generous period of 14 days, plus extra-time. So go ahead and sign-up today, it's absolutely Free and who knows, you just may score one of your favorite purses you didn't know you loved!

Purchase Tokens

Acquire any of our token packages and get ready to participate in the auction. Every bid you place will add only 0.10 cents to the current price of the item. Remember to place a bid at least once within 14 days to gain access to the extra-time. This additional time period is crucial since the user who places the final bid during this period will emerge as the winner of the auction. Tokens can be purchased with either a credit card or PayPal and are non refundable. You can use the coins towards bidding, or towards purchases from our store which hosts a series of new exciting items each month.

Auction Bids

The auction will be open for a period of 2 weeks, allowing all registered users to submit offers using their tokens. If a bid is placed within the final 10 seconds of the auction, an additional period will be activated. Each subsequent bid during this period will reset the timer to 10 seconds, with a maximum extension time of either 5, 10, 15, 30, 60, 90, 120, or 180 minutes. Only users who have previously submitted at least one bid within the two-week auction period will be permitted to continue bidding during the extension period. The final price displayed will be paid by the winning bidder.

Win Your Items

To participate in the auction and increase your chances of winning, it's important to sign in and place your bid before the start of extra-time. With each new bid, the timer resets to 10 seconds, which gives other bidders the opportunity to make a counter-offer. Extra-time can last anywhere between 5 to a maximum of 180 minutes. The highest bidder at the end of the extra-time period wins the auction, and the final price displayed will be the amount the winner needs to pay. One thing to note is that we always offer free shipping!
